Robert Dowd

Data Scientist

Denver, CO

One of my favorite quotes, spoken by philospher Jason Silva, is: 'Creativity and insight almost always involve an experience of acute pattern recognition: the eureka moment in which we perceive the interconnection between disparate concepts or ideas to reveal something new.' This quote has not only helped me understand what it is that makes me so excited about data science, but also given me inspiration to pursue the career that I can love. I am currently seeking employment opportunities to continue chasing my goals of becoming a data scientist, and those creative experiences ending in a eureka moment.

Some of my interests in the world of data science include machine learning, robotics and AI, healthcare data, biological, public health data analysis, and more. While finding experiences in these areas, I hope to draw on what skills and strengths I have gained thus far, while developing new passions along the way.

Outside of data science, I am a very active person, and the great outdoors is often where you'll find me. I love to run, hike the Colorado 14ers, play tennis and row. Additionally, I have a passion for photography that I maintain on the side, documenting my adventures, passions, and the simple life of my adorable cat Finn.
